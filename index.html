Projects

Web-Based Inventory System

For our System Analysis and Development subject, our team searched for a locale to figure out what project to make. We found a water refilling business then conduct an interview. The business-owner uses paper-based inventory to keep track of everything. Some of the problems they encounter are: (1) the pile of papers makes things harder to organize, (2) manual computation of sales takes time, and (3) it's hard to figure out who has the gallons, causing the business to lose some of them.

Our team was able to present our concept and was approved by the professor. We then created a prototype of it as our project for our web developement subject. Our plan is to continue the project for our thesis, however, due to multiple unfortunate circumstances, the plan has changed. Firstly, the pandemic made things difficult. Second, the group was separated to different sections, making the members join other groups. Lastly, we found out that the inventory system will not be approved as our capstone project.

OBRA: A Website for Blue-Collar Workers in Tarlac City

With a new group, and a pandemic and lockdown going on, the members came up with a project that does not require the team to go out interviewing people. The group settled for a website where blue-collar workers can register and offer their service and clients can request for them. For scalability, the project was limited to people at Tarlac city only.

The website was named obra because it means "work" in pampango or kapampangan, one of the prominent languages used at the province of Tarlac.

Employees Performance Management System

This is my first project which is not an academic requirement. The system can be managed by multiple admins. In the system, the managers rates the employees' performance from 0 to 10 based on certain criterias that are provided by the system such as: attendance, attitude, and work quality. The scores are then computed and tallied.
